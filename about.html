About Prepify

Helpful tips for easier meal prep

Prepify is designed to make weekly food planning feel calmer, more visual, and easier to stick with.

How To Use Prepify

  • Browse recipes that match your time, mood, and meal goals.
  • Add favourite meals into your weekly planner.
  • Turn your meal choices into a practical grocery list.

Quick Tips

  • Choose 2 or 3 flexible proteins for the week.
  • Repeat one easy breakfast to simplify prep.
  • Leave one meal open for leftovers or a treat.
